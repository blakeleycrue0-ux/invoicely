FAQ

Common questions

Do I need to pay to use Invoicely?
No. You get a free account with 2 PDF downloads included. To unlock unlimited downloads it's a one-time payment of $2.99 — no subscription, ever.
Are my documents saved?
Yes. Every invoice and quote is saved to your account automatically. You can access them from any device, edit them, download them again and track their payment status.
Can I use my own logo and branding?
Absolutely. Upload your logo and choose where it appears on the document. You can also set custom background colors, currencies, bank details and footer messages.
Does the revenue dashboard track my income?
Yes. When you mark an invoice as Paid, it counts toward your total revenue and monthly earnings. The dashboard shows your revenue over time with charts.
Does it work on my phone?
Yes — Invoicely works on any device including iPhone, Android, tablet and laptop. Create an invoice on your phone and it's saved to your account ready to access anywhere.
